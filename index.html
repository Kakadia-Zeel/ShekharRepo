About Us

We are Captain Steeltech Company into Bathroom Accessories & Sanitary Ware Trading activities since 1968 and serving the Distribution and retail sector perfectly.
Looking at the market demand and development in Bathroom Accessories sector we decide to manufacture the same and into Manufacturing sector since last 4 Years now.
Before entering into Manufacturing we gave lot of thoughts to various design aspects of Bathroom Accessories. In the process we enquired about the requirements of customers (end users). Then we asked the wholesalers and retailers about the changes they want to see in Bathroom Accessories.
